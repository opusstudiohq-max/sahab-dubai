Our Story · حكايتنا

From the Levant,
with fire.

SAHAB — Arabic for cloud — is a love letter to the Levant, served fifty-two floors above Downtown Dubai. Chef Karim Haddad carries the flavours of Aleppo and Beirut into the modern age: charcoal, saffron, orange blossom, and smoke.

Every plate begins over open fire. Every evening ends above the clouds.
